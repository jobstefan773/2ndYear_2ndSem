This follows the story of Chihiro, a young girl who gets trapped in a mystical spirit world while moving to a new neighborhood with her parents. To save her family, she must work at a bathhouse owned by the powerful witch Yubaba. Along the way, Chihiro encounters various spirits, learns valuable lessons, and discovers her inner strength. With the help of her friends, she confronts Yubaba and secures her family's freedom, ultimately returning to the human world.
